about our

tea Spot

Hi, I am Margrette Brown. The owner of Tea Spot. I started Tea Spot in 2011 when she discovered how drinking tea a day changed my life. The health benefits I gain from drinking tea changed my life for the better. I noticed my stress level was down. I had more energy than ever before and I felt a the relaxation that I have never felt in years.

And I want to share that with people. I want to help others relieve their stresses and give them more energy through my tea.

Tea Spot is a family own business located in downtown Nashville Tennessee. We have been providing Tennesseans with refresh, soothing, delicious tea for 10yrs.
